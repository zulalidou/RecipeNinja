About Us

Have you ever wanted to know how a certain food was put together? Have you ever wanted to know what magical ingredients were used to create that delicious meal you recently had at your favorite restaurant? Have you ever wanted to simply level up your culinary skills? Well, that's what we're here for! At foodconnoisseur, we make it OUR BUSINESS to answer these questions for you.

At its core, foodconnoisseur is an educational and informative website about any food that you can think of! We provide a simple, yet extensive collection of food recipes to anyone, regardless of your experience level dealing with food. Whether you’re an experienced chef, a home cook, a gastronomy student, a food fanatic, or just an average joe, you’ll definitely find something here that piques the interest of the little (or massive) foodie within you.

DETAILED RECIPE BREAKDOWNS

Wanna know how much butter you need to put into that pumpkin risotto? No problem, we’ve got you covered! Wanna know what kinds of nutrients (and in what amount) are in those chili cheese tacos that you just made? We’ve got a handy list for you to go through for that! Wanna know exactly what equipments you need at your disposal before making that sweet hummingbird cake you’ve been craving all week? Well.. you get the idea.

We provide you with a detailed recipe breakdown for whatever there is to know about a recipe. Be it the recipe’s diet list, its nutrient list, its detailed instructions, or (..), we’ve got it all here for you!

NO DATA COLLECTION

We do not (under any circumstances) collect any of our users’ data. We understand that YOUR data is something that belongs to YOU and YOU alone, and we fully respect that.

Now that you’ve learned about who we are, what are you waiting for? Help nurture and foster the foodconnoisseur within you, and start browsing through our extensive food collection to learn how to cook your favorite meals!
